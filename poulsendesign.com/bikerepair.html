Bike Repair

In early August of 2024, my bicycle of 12 years was stolen outside of my apartment. The bike was a gift from my dad for my 12th birthday, and I didn't have any plans to part with it. I had left the bike locked outside with just a cable lock, and the next morning it was gone.

So there I was, left with a cut cable lock and no bicycle. Luckily, a previous roommate left their old, unused junk bicycle here, and I thought it might be a good opportunity to fix it up.

I had zero prior experience working on bicycles, and over the course of the next 16 hours I learned how to inspect the bike to see what repairs it needed, how to replace brake cables and housing, how to replace cantilever brakes, how to replace grip shifters, etc.
